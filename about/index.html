About Adam Freilich

I'm a student at Penn persuing Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Math expecting to graduate in December of 2014. I'm also minoring in Computer Science and Logic, Information & Computation.

I'm particularly interested in Logic and how it relates to computation. I was particularly inspired by applications of finite model theory to complexity theory. I'm also interested in algorithms, algebra, category theory, machine learning, programming languages and topology.

I'm currently applying to Ph.D. programs (with the intent of starting in Fall 2015).
